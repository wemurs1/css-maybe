The Protagonist

The Design:

The colour palette I chose reflects a dark and dull colour scheme, a large contrast to the vibrance of the enemies and eventual scenery, presenting how someone who was once vibrant had fallen into themselves and into darkness. The character himself was created based off 'The Living Tombstone' character, 'skull guy', a somewhat consistent motive of the channel, a design I had been interested in making my own adaptation for a while.
