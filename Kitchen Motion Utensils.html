Kitchen Motion Utensils

The Concept:

In this second group project, I had been set with the task to create an item that would in some form fit our audience target. In this particular project, we created a knife with the ability to collect points in order to lessen your grocery shopping struggles with an inbuilt feature to detect motion to level up every time you use it.

The app/how it works

The app itself is designed to link to the tool to remind users to use the item and level up with each usage to unlock discounts and tokens for local grocery stores. Through this, the gamification aspect works to encourage people to cook more frequently to hopefully make users more healthy and send them recipes that are cheap and easy to follow.
